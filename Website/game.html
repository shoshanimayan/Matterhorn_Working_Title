Character Bios

Roddie Raccoon

It’s me! The coolest Raccoon there is! I’m a rad dude with lots of attitude. Are you a bad enough dude to collect trash? Let’s go on a rad quest to collect trash and machine parts to find out! Fight enemies! And complete the mad money infinite trash generator machine!

Likes: I love corn dogs and road trips across middle America
Dislikes: uncool things, homework, textbooks, school, your mom telling you to clean your room

Forager Rabbit

Forager Rabbits are huge babies! Every time I get near one of these identical siblings they start running away! They’re pretty lame and not at all tubular, but they’re always holding some goodies on em, beat them up! Take their stuff!

Likes: their mommy because they’re all huge babies!
Dislikes: hanging out with cool raccoons like me. Their loss

Craig Coyote

Craig is a wiley jerk! This sneaky son of a gun always wants to get into a fight! He’s still angry about that time I stole all his food and he had to starve all winter, let it go dude. If he sees you, he’ll rush at you and try to beat you up! Luckily this doggo is more bark than bite, and he doesn’t put up much of a fight, and you can beat him up for his lunch.

Likes: picking fights, being a jerk, chasing birds, being a jerk, Led Zeppelin, AND BEING A JERK.
Dislikes: being told he’s a jerk, having his stuff stolen I guess, losing.

Mob Moles

These guys are mobsters I guess? They say they’re all in the mafia, but I’ve never really see them do anything until recently, and it kinda feels like they just watch to many movies. I guess their boss is upset with me or something, and they’ve been trying to pick a fight with me, lame. Despite being blind, these moles have weirdly good aim, and they’ll use it to throw balls of mud (I hope its mud at least) at you, and they’ll hide underground where you can’t hit them.

Likes: Mob movies, talking in a fake mobster voice, Chicago Style Gangster Accents, tea.
Dislikes: being made fun off, getting beat up, being told to stop hanging out in the dirt, taking baths.
